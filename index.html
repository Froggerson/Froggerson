Our World is dying...

Let's save it. Together

So what's up?

Let's get this out of the way: climate change is happening right now, and if we don't something, its effects will have a negative effect on the future. It can be scary to go about and change things by ourselves, but luckily for us UCSD offers a bunch of organizations that will help students "bend the curve". Now is the perfect time to Let's find a community that will support you and our world!

Would simply joining a club actually help?

Of course it will! Even if you do not actively participate in fighting against climate change right now, finding an group that does will help you gain awareness about strategies that will make a difference. You don't need to participate in a rally or lead a protest to reverse the effects of climate change; you can teach others, make small changes to your lifestyle, get your friends involved, or even talk about it casually in conversation. Now is the perfect time to do that, and nothing is better than meeting new people who believe in the same causes as you do! Let's take a look at what UCSD has to offer.
